1. Prepare Canadian Computing Competition – Junior level

For grade 6-12 students, students don’t need programming experience.

This course aims to prepare for the annual competition organized by the Waterloo University. The top 20 Senior Level contestants will be invited to participate in the Canadian Computing Olympiad (CCO) competition. The results of both competitions are used to determine Canadian Team members to participate in the International Olympiad of Informatics (IOI).
